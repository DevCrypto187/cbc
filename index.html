About Us

who we are

Founded in 2023, ChainBridge Capital is a Taiwan-based global venture and investment firm focused on the Web3 space. With over $50 million in assets under management (AUM), we specialize in supporting foundational blockchain projects like Layer 1 blockchains and DeFi ecosystems. We are committed to fostering long-term growth by leveraging our global network, strategic guidance, and deep market research. By bridging blockchain innovation with traditional finance, we empower projects to redefine the digital economy and drive lasting impact.

Investment Philosophy

At ChainBridge Capital, we believe in identifying and supporting projects that are shaping the future of blockchain and finance. Our comprehensive evaluation process focuses on:

  • Technological Innovation: Groundbreaking solutions in scalability, interoperability, and security.
  • Market Potential: Real-world adoption and ability to address key challenges.
  • Team Excellence: Visionary leaders with proven expertise and resilience.
  • Tokenomics & Sustainability: Long-term economic viability and stakeholder alignment.
  • Regulatory Viability: Clear strategies for navigating evolving regulations.
  • Strategic Fit: Alignment with our mission and portfolio synergy.
